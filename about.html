About

This website is the result of a bachelorprojekt with the focus on making a catalog of usability problems of the web. On this web site the usability catalog will be presented. Here you will find several sections of web usability problems every section has its own definition and problems listed under it that fit the description of the section. These sections also have a title and examples listed with them to further describe them. Every problem listed under these sections will have its own title and a description of the problem. The main goal of the catalog is to log and define the usability problems of the web and have a pace to find them in the future.

If you have a usability problem on the web that is not listed or if you have any further questions about the projekt do not hesitate to contact the authors.
